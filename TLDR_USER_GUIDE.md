## ðŸš€ The Workflow (How to Pilot)

### PHASE 1: THE ORACLE (The Interview)
*   **Goal:** Turn your vague idea into a "Project Bible."
*   **Action:** Click the **Crystal Ball**.
*   **Don't say:** "Build a twitter clone."
*   **Do say:** "I need a scalable architecture for a micro-blogging platform focusing on high-ingest rates."
*   **Magic:** The Oracle will grill you with questions. Answer them. Then click **"Perform Context Fusion"**.

### PHASE 2: GENESIS (The Constitution)
*   **Goal:** Establish the Laws.
*   **Action:** The system generates a `Constitution`.
*   **Conflict Check:** If you asked for "Python" but selected a "React Template," the engine will stop you.
*   **Pro Tip:** Add `NEGATIVE CONSTRAINT: DO NOT USE TAILWIND` in your prompt to force specific exclusions.

### PHASE 3: THE PRISM (The Decomposer)
*   **Goal:** Break the project into ~20-50 Atomic Tasks.
*   **Action:** The Prism classifies your domain (e.g., "FinTech," "Game Dev") and spawns a **Council of Specialists**.
*   **Your Job:** Review the Task List. Toggle OFF anything you don't need.

### PHASE 4: THE FACTORY (Production)
*   **Goal:** Execute the plan, one Brick at a time.
*   **The Loop:**
    1.  **Specialist:** Writes the spec.
    2.  **Reflexion:** Critiques itself immediately.
    3.  **Surveyor:** Checks for "AI Slop" (hedging, refusals).
    4.  **Antagonist:** Tries to destroy the brick with logic.
    5.  **Masonry:** If it survives, it is cemented into the `Session Codex`.

## ðŸ’¡ Troubleshooting "The Ghost in the Machine"

*   **"All Heads Severed"**:
    *   Your API Key is dead/limited. Check `Settings -> Model Configuration`.
*   **"The Loop of Death" (Reject -> Repair -> Reject)**:
    *   The Antagonist is too strict for the model. **Lower Antagonist settings** or **Increase Model Intelligence** (switch to Sonnet/GPT-4).
*   **"Zero Tasks Generated"**:
    *   JSON parse error. **Refresh the Page**. State is saved in IndexedDB; a refresh clears the bad context.
